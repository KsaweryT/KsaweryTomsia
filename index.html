About me

Currently, I am a master's Data Science student at Kozminski University in Poland. Passionate about what the future, and machine learning, are going to bring to us!

After 10 years of my journey with volleyball and earning my bachelor's at California State University in the US, I decided to invest in myself and do something more fascinating than volleyball, Data Science! While I still regularly check what is going on in the world of sports, I spend most of my time on learning, coding or on hobbies.

I am interested in contemporary philosophy, especially in works of Michael Foucault that I find very explanatory to lots of societal problems of the modern world. Besides coding and working with data, I also like to relax watching documentaries or reading history. One of my favorite books is a few thousand years old book, Bhagavad Gita. As for programming, I am a type of person that finds working with code very pleasurable.
