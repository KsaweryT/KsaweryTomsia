Extracting, transforming and loading the data of one of the Polish stores.

In this project, I was faced with a difficult to read excel file, that was hard to read. In my work, I have created a code that goes through files in our computer, and reads these files automatically into one, well-organized data file that is much easier to read.

The automated code, allows us to quickly read the data, and analyze it. The code is independent of new inputs, and creates all of the graphs, in a clean manner, that is free of any additional work. I also performed simple regressions, that are easy to interpret, and just as the graphs, independent of new input.

Data Analysis

In my analysis, in addition to graphs and regressions, I performed a few t-tests in order to further understand the data. The conclusions:

  • 99% of all stores' revenues comes from only one type of flower!
  • The difference in revenues between the months does not differ.
  • The location of the store, in terms of location's population, does not influence the store's revenues.
  • The stores in the Southern Poland do better than these in the Northern Poland.
